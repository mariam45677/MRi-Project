Tomotherapy

Painless and effective
cancer treatment with
the most advanced technology

Reduced
side effects

Instead of treating tumours in different parts of the body separately, Tomotherapy is applied those parts simultaneously, without the need of division for large areas.Integrated imaging and advanced dose-delivery capabilities enable your treatment team to create a more personalized treatment plan and confidently deliver the most precise dose of radiation, directly to the tumor.

Accurate
treatment

High precision minimizes dose to surrounding healthy tissues, which can significantly reduce the most common side effects of radiation therapy such as fatigue or skin problems.

Painless
treatment

Radixact® technology targets exclusively the tumour with extremely high precision, while protecting the healthy tissues around it. Therefore, protecting healthy tissues plays an important role in increasing the life quality, including the long-term survival by minimizing the risk of cancer recurrence.

What is the Radixact®
System?

The Radixact System is a singular radiation
therapy system that effectively treats cancer
anywhere in the body with high precision.
The only radiation therapy system designed on a CT
scanner foundation, the Radixact System uses
daily imaging to guide the treatment each day
and deliver highly accurate radiation dose from
360 degrees around the patient.

A distinctive feature of this system is the new generation of spiral
radiation therapy. In addition, the device combines a linear gas pedal
and a computed tomograph for precise visualization of the tumor.

Double multi-blade collimators located on different sides are capable
of conducting a comprehensive 360-degree irradiation of the target,
directing the radiation precisely within the specified limits and adjusting
the radiation dose if necessary. Consultation
